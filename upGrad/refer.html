Frequently Asked Questions

ELIGIBILITY expand_more

Do I need to be an upGrad student to refer my friends?

add
No, every user who has signed up on upGrad can be a part of upGrad’s reward program. Benefits vary for a paid and non-paid user as per the terms and conditions.

Who is an enrolled student and who is not an enrolled student?

add
Students who have only signed up on upGrad website and have not paid any amount are not enrolled students while the ones who have paid for an upGrad course are enrolled students.
HOW TO USE expand_more

How can I refer my friends/connections?

add
Sign up for upGrad’s reward program. You can refer your friends by entering their email addresses one at a time or by sharing your referral links via social media.

What does my friend have to do for the referral benefit?

add
Your friend needs to apply the referral code at the application stage for both of you to be eligible for referral benefits.
PAYOUTS expand_more

When will I be eligible for the gift cards/payouts?

add
Whenever your friend makes the full payment with us and uses your referral code while filling the application, you will be eligible for the upGrad reward, i.e, referral benefits. Post that, the upGrad team will be responsible for checking your eligibility and making the payouts.

How do I get my gift cards/vouchers?

add
For the paid students: Once the referee makes the full payment, you will be eligible to receive the referral benefits/payouts. You should receive the referral benefit/payouts within 15 days from your referee's batch commencement date.
